About Us

The creative company, Digital Beehive, was created to help your business with your public image and branding. Within our company we have a selection of bright and creative young people with a range of different skills and abilities, from branding & graphic design to animation & web development to help fufill whatever your company's needs may be. As digital natives we specialise and excel in helping businesses leverage social networks with our specific skills sets & knowledge.


We were started as part of WIP Week at Plymouth University. During which we created the products shown here for clients such as Plymouth University itself and a soon to be launched company called Gimea. It was a valuable experience for the whole team and enabled us to get both industry insight as well as being given the chance to work on our own creative skills and meet some amazing new people.
